About Event

Know More About The Event

The Cyber Shield Summit & Pixelverse Premier is a groundbreaking event merging cybersecurity and cutting-edge digital innovation. It highlights advancements in cybersecurity, Web3, and metaverse technologies, featuring expert panels, workshops, and interactive showcases. Topics include AI-driven threat intelligence, blockchain security, NFTs, gaming, and virtual reality. Attendees can engage in hands-on sessions, hackathons, and discussions on safeguarding digital ecosystems while exploring the future of immersive tech. This event fosters collaboration among developers, artists, and industry leaders, bridging the gap between secure digital transformation and creative innovation, making it a hub for networking and technological growth.

Whence at the Conference

CodeRedX 2K25 is a premier conference by WHOAMICON, uniting developers, ethical hackers, and AI researchers. It explores open-source collaboration, AI advancements, and digital identity security through expert talks, hands-on sessions, and networking. Attendees gain insights into DevOps, AI ethics, cybersecurity, and blockchain innovations shaping the future.
